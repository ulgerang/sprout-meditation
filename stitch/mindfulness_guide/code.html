The Art of
Sitting

Meditation is not about stopping your thoughts, but rather learning how to observe them without judgment.

self_improvement

Posture

Find a comfortable seat. Keep your spine straight but relaxed, imagining a string pulling you gently from the crown of your head like a stack of golden coins.

air

Breathing

Breathe naturally. Don't force it. Simply observe the sensation of cool air entering and warm air leaving your nose. Let the breath be your anchor.

filter_drama

Mindset

Thoughts will come. Let them pass like clouds drifting across a vast blue sky. Gently, without self-criticism, return your focus to the breath.
